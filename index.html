Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Do I need to move my lessons to your platform?

No. ChessBooker handles booking and scheduling only. You still teach wherever you prefer—Zoom, Google Meet, Lichess, Chess.com, or any other tool.

Can I still use Zoom, Google Meet, or Lichess?

Absolutely. ChessBooker doesn't replace your teaching tools. Once a lesson is booked, you send your link and use whatever setup works for you.

What if a student cancels?

You set your own cancellation policy. Students see your policy before booking, so expectations are always clear from the start.

Do you take a cut of my lessons?

Our goal is a simple, predictable fee structure—not a percentage of every lesson. Early access coaches will get the best rates.

Can I sell lesson packs?

Yes, that's on our roadmap. You'll be able to offer bundles so students can commit and you can plan ahead.

Is this for beginners only?

Not at all. ChessBooker works for coaches teaching any level—from kids learning the rules to club players preparing for tournaments.

Can parents book for kids?

Yes. Parents can book and pay on behalf of their children. The confirmation email goes to whichever address is used at checkout.
