The Full Lesson Lifecycle

Every step from request to completion, handled automatically.

A

Student sends a request

  • Student fills out your booking form with their name, email, and timezone
  • They pick preferred times and session length
  • Request goes straight to your dashboard for review
B

You accept the lesson

  • One click to confirm the booking
  • Google Calendar event is created automatically
  • Google Meet link is generated and attached
  • Both you and the student receive confirmation emails
C

Reschedule if needed

  • Pick a new date and time from your dashboard
  • Google Calendar event updates automatically
  • Meet link stays the same — no confusion
  • Reschedule emails sent to both parties
D

Cancel when necessary

  • Cancel a session with one click
  • Google Calendar event is removed automatically
  • Cancellation emails notify the student
  • Your booking still updates even if Google is down

Google Calendar & Meet

Connect once, automate forever.

1

Connect your Google account once

One click from your Settings page. We only ask for calendar access — not your email or other data.

2

Your data stays secure

Your Google credentials are stored securely and never exposed. You can disconnect anytime.

3

Events created automatically

Accept a booking → calendar event with Meet link appears instantly. Reschedule or cancel → the event updates or deletes automatically.
