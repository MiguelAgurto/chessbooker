How ChessBooker Works

Students request lessons. You confirm or decline. Meeting links are created automatically. No more back-and-forth messages.

Join the Pilot

For Students

A simple, professional booking experience in three steps.

1

Visit your booking page

Each coach has a public booking page. Students see your availability, rates, and lesson options all in one place.

  • Your profile and coaching details
  • Available time slots in the student's timezone
  • Session lengths you offer
Screenshot: Public Booking Page
Clean booking form with coach profile and availability
2

Submit a lesson request

Students pick their preferred times and submit a request. No account required. Just name, email, and timezone.

  • Select preferred time slots
  • Choose session duration
  • Add optional notes for the coach
Screenshot: Request Form
Simple form with time selection and student details
3

Get confirmed and join

Once you confirm the lesson, students receive an email with all the details and a single "Join lesson" button when it's time.

  • Confirmation email with lesson details
  • Calendar invite (if you use Google Calendar)
  • One-click access to join the video call
Screenshot: Upcoming Lessons
Student view with "Join lesson" button

For Coaches

Stay in control of your schedule. Confirm lessons on your terms.

Review pending requests

When a student requests a lesson, it appears in your dashboard. You decide whether to confirm or decline.

  • See student details and requested times
  • Confirm with one click
  • Decline if the time doesn't work
  • Students are notified either way
Screenshot: Confirm / Decline Request
Pending request with student info and action buttons

Automatic meeting links

When you confirm a lesson, ChessBooker creates a Google Calendar event with a Google Meet link automatically. No extra steps.

Not using Google? Enter your own Zoom, Discord, or other meeting link when confirming.

  • Google Calendar + Meet created automatically
  • Manual link option for Zoom, Discord, or others
  • Meeting link visible to both you and the student
