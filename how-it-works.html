Common Questions

Do students book exact time slots?
Yes. Students see the specific times you've made available and pick one. No vague "preferred windows" — they choose a real slot.
What if I don't use Google Calendar?
No problem. When confirming a lesson, you can enter your own meeting link — Zoom, Discord, or anything else. Google Calendar is optional.
Does ChessBooker generate a Google Meet link?
Yes, when your Google account is connected. When you confirm a lesson, ChessBooker creates a calendar event with a Meet link automatically.
How do timezones work?
Students see available times in their own timezone. You set your availability in your timezone. ChessBooker handles the conversion automatically.
Can I send lesson recaps?
Yes. After a lesson, you can write notes and send a recap email to the student with what you covered and next steps.
